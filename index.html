@zachfeldman

Zach Feldman is a technologist now and has been a tinkerer from childhood. Most recently, he was a Lead Software Engineer at SpaceX, and before that was Vice President of Technology for Getaway House, responsible for the company's online presence and cabin automation projects. He develops websites and applications lately using Python, Flask, React, Docker, Ruby, Rails, Sinatra, and plain old HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

When he's not writing code, you can find Zach playing his guitar, DJing a party, skiing a slope, or exploring a new place.
