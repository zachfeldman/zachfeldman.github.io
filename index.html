@zachfeldman

Zach Feldman is a technologist now and has been a tinkerer from childhood. He's currently a Staff Software Engineer on the Marketplace team at Framework, primarily working in Ruby on Rails and Solidus. Previously, he was a Lead Software Engineer at SpaceX on flight software CI/build/code review systems and the flight data review application. Before that, he was Vice President of Technology for Getaway House, responsible for the company's online booking system and cabin automation projects. He develops websites and applications lately using Ruby, Rails, Solidus, Tailwind, and of course plain old HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

When he's not writing code, you can find Zach playing his guitar, DJing a party, skiing a slope, or exploring a new place.
